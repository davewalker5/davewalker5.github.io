A bower was a temporary structure made of branches.

## Field Description

Constructed from fresh oak boughs on elevated ground.

## In White’s Landscape

Rebuilt each year at Waldon and Brimstone Lodges.

## Notes

Its survival lies in the tradition of rebuilding, not the structure itself.
