“Malm” describes a soft, pale, workable soil.

## Field Description

Crumbly, light-coloured earth, often easily cultivated compared to harder chalk.

## In White’s Landscape

Malm soils supported agriculture and formed gentler slopes between more resistant geological layers.

## Notes

The contrast between malm and “rag” reflects practical land use as much as geology.
