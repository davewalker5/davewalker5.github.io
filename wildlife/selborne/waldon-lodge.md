Waldon Lodge was a site on high ground within Woolmer Forest.

## Field Description

A “conspicuous eminence” offering wide views across the heath.

## In White’s Landscape

One of two locations where bowers were maintained.

## Notes

The structure is gone, but the topography remains identifiable.
