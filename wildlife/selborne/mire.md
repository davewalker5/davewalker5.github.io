Mires are wet depressions within the heath.

## Field Description

Waterlogged ground with:

- mosses
- sedges
- standing water

## In White’s Landscape

Sources of peat and ecological diversity.

## Notes

Important habitats, often overlooked in broader descriptions of the heath.
