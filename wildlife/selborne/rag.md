“Rag” refers to a hard, irregular stone found in the Selborne area.

## Field Description

Rubbly, broken-textured stone, often encountered beneath sandy soils or in banks.

## In White’s Landscape

Used as a local building material and forming part of the geological structure beneath the heath.

## Notes

Likely part of the Upper Greensand formation.
