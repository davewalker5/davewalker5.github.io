Heathland is the defining habitat of Woolmer Forest.

## Field Description

A mosaic of:
- dwarf shrubs (heather)
- spiny shrubs (gorse)
- grasses and bracken

## In White’s Landscape

Managed through:
- grazing
- cutting
- controlled burning

## Notes

A semi-natural habitat—dependent on human management for its persistence.
