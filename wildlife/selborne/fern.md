“Fern” in White’s usage generally refers to bracken.

## Field Description

Tall, spreading fronds forming dense cover in summer.

## In White’s Landscape

Common on disturbed or marginal ground within the heath.

## Notes

Highly flammable when dry; significant in controlled burning regimes.
