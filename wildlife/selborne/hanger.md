The Hanger is the steep wooded escarpment above Selborne.

## Field Description

A sharp slope rising from the village, often wooded and shaded.

## In White’s Landscape

A contrast to the open heath of Woolmer Forest.

## Notes

“Hanger” is a regional term for steep woodland on chalk slopes.
