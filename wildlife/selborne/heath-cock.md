The “heath cock” is the male **black grouse**.

## Field Description

A medium-sized grouse with black plumage, red combs, and a distinctive lyre-shaped tail.

## In White’s Landscape

Once a characteristic bird of the heath around Selborne.

## Today

No longer present in southern England.

## Notes

An example of how species distributions have shifted since White’s time.
