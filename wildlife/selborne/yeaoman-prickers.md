Yeoman prickers were mounted men employed in deer drives.

## Field Description

They rode at distance, guiding and containing deer rather than chasing directly.

## In White’s Landscape

Part of the operational structure of the royal forest at Woolmer.

## Notes

“Pricking” refers to urging movement, likely derived from the use of spurs.
