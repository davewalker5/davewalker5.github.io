“Burning lime” refers to the production of quicklime in a kiln.

## Process

Limestone (chalk) is heated to produce quicklime, later used in:
- soil improvement
- mortar and plaster

## In White’s Landscape

Fuel came from the heath:
- gorse
- turf
- peat

## Notes

A small-scale but essential rural industry embedded in the landscape.
