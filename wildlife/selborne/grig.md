“Grig” is an old dialect term referring to coarse, wiry grasses.

## Field Description

Fine, often dry grasses growing between heather and gorse, forming the base layer of the heath.

## In White’s Landscape

Though less conspicuous than shrubs, this layer:
- carries fire quickly
- recovers rapidly after burning
- contributes to the structure of the habitat

## Notes

A subtle but important component of heath ecology—easy to overlook, but essential to the system.
