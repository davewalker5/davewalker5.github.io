Stromatolite Growth Modeller

Exploring the emergence of stromatolite structure through computational ecology

The models described throughout these investigations are generated using a computational framework that simulates the interaction between microbial growth, photosynthesis, sediment deposition, burial, and changing environmental conditions.

Rather than prescribing layered structures directly, the model allows lamination to emerge from biologically interpretable processes represented by coupled differential equations and modular environmental forcing.

Beginning with a one-dimensional proof of concept, the project explores how relatively simple ecological interactions can reproduce many of the characteristic features preserved in stromatolites, while providing a flexible foundation for more sophisticated future models.

The complete source code, example notebooks, documentation, and instructions for running the modeller are available in the GitHub repository.
