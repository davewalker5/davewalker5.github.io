## Aim
To compare field size and visible detail at different magnifications.

## Specimen & Apparatus
- Stage micrometer slide
- Prepared slide (any specimen)
- Compound microscope with multiple objectives

## Method
1. Focus the stage micrometer under low power.
2. Observe the number of divisions visible across the field.
3. Change to a higher power objective and refocus.
4. Compare the visible field size and clarity of the scale.
5. Repeat the process using a prepared specimen.

## Observations to Make
- Change in field diameter with increasing magnification.
- Increase in visible detail at higher powers.
- Reduction in depth of field.
- Any change in brightness or ease of focusing.
