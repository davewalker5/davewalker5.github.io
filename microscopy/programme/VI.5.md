## Aim
To study microorganisms present in soil suspensions.

## Specimen & Apparatus
- Garden soil
- Water
- Small container
- Pipette or dropper
- Slide and coverslip
- Compound microscope

## Method
1. Mix a small quantity of soil with water in a container.
2. Allow heavier particles to settle briefly.
3. Transfer a drop of the suspension to a slide.
4. Apply a coverslip carefully.
5. Examine under low and then higher magnification.

## Observations to Make
- Variety of organisms present.
- Differences in size and movement.
- Distribution of organisms within the sample.
- Presence of particulate matter and organic debris.
