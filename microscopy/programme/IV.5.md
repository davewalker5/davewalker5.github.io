## Aim
To examine the structure and movement of rotifers.

## Specimen & Apparatus
- Pond water with plant debris
- Pipette or dropper
- Slide and coverslip
- Compound microscope

## Method
1. Transfer a drop of water containing debris to a slide.
2. Apply a coverslip carefully.
3. Examine under low power to locate organisms.
4. Increase magnification to observe structure and movement.
5. Adjust illumination to improve contrast.

## Observations to Make
- Body shape and segmentation.
- Movement and feeding behaviour.
- Presence of rotating structures at the head.
- Interaction with surrounding particles.
