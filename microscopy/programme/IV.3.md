## Aim
To study the form and structure of diatom frustules.

## Specimen & Apparatus
- Pond water or scrapings from wet stone
- Pipette or needle
- Slide and coverslip
- Water
- Compound microscope

## Method
1. Place a drop of the sample on a slide.
2. Apply a coverslip carefully.
3. Examine under low power to locate diatoms.
4. Increase magnification to observe detail.
5. Adjust illumination to enhance contrast.

## Observations to Make
- Shape and symmetry of diatoms.
- Presence of fine surface markings.
- Variation in form between individuals.
- Clarity of detail under different illumination.
