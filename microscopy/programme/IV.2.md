## Aim
To observe the structure of filamentous algae.

## Specimen & Apparatus
- Filamentous algae from pond edge or wet surface
- Forceps or pipette
- Slide and coverslip
- Water
- Compound microscope

## Method
1. Collect a small portion of algae.
2. Place on a slide with a drop of water.
3. Arrange the filaments so they lie flat.
4. Apply a coverslip carefully.
5. Examine under low and then higher magnification.

## Observations to Make
- Arrangement of cells within filaments.
- Shape and size of individual cells.
- Presence of internal structures.
- Any variation along the filament.
