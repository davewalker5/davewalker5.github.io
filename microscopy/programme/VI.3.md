## Aim
To study the structures associated with fungal spore production.

## Specimen & Apparatus
- Mould (e.g. on citrus peel)
- Needle or mounted pin
- Slide and coverslip
- Water
- Compound microscope

## Method
1. Collect a small portion of mould using a needle.
2. Transfer to a slide with a drop of water.
3. Tease apart gently to expose structures.
4. Apply a coverslip carefully.
5. Examine under low and then higher magnification.

## Observations to Make
- Presence of spore-bearing structures.
- Arrangement of spores on supporting filaments.
- Shape and size of spores.
- Differences between vegetative and reproductive structures.
