## Aim
To observe the structure and form of plant hairs (trichomes).

## Specimen & Apparatus
- Leaf (e.g. tomato, geranium, or nettle)
- Forceps or needle
- Slide and coverslip
- Water
- Compound microscope

## Method
1. Remove a small portion of the leaf bearing visible hairs.
2. Place directly on a slide in a drop of water.
3. Apply a coverslip gently.
4. Examine under low power to locate hairs.
5. Increase magnification to observe structure.

## Observations to Make
- Shape and size of trichomes.
- Whether hairs are simple or branched.
- Distribution across the leaf surface.
- Any internal structure visible within the hairs.
