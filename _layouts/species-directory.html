This directory brings together seasonal analyses for species recorded in {{ page.location }} where there is enough observational data to describe a meaningful pattern through the year.

Each species account uses long-term field records to explore its seasonal character: when it is most often encountered, how sharply its activity or visibility changes through the year, and whether its pattern suggests residency, migration, a flowering period, or an adult butterfly flight period.

The directory is intended as a reference companion to the more selective *Year in the Life* analyses. Rather than presenting a curated seasonal narrative, it provides a broader set of species accounts across birds, butterflies and flowering plants.

The classifications are interpretive rather than absolute. They describe the seasonal shape of the observations in this landscape, supported by fitted curves and model-derived summaries. Species with too little data are not included.
